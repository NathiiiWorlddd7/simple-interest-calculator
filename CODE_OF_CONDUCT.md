# Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive community. All participants are expected to abide by this Code of Conduct.

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good intent and seek to resolve conflicts constructively.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

This project adheres to the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) Code of Conduct. Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers.

Enforcement guidelines follow the recommendations in the Contributor Covenant. The maintainers will review and take appropriate action as needed.
